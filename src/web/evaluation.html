HOW TO EVALUATE FILM BUDDY

  1. Go to www.filmbuddy.eu and log in with Facebook
  2. You will be redirected to a page, which contains a list of words. These words represent your interests, based on your Facebook posts and likes. Feel free to add or remove interests and click Submit.
  3. You will be redirected to a page, which contains the recommended movies for you. Please interact with the available filters (top-left button), so that results best suit your tastes. Feel free to click on any movie to see more details.
  4. Click “Feedback” from the top menu to get redirected to a questionnaire about your experience. At the “Recommendation Ratings” section of the questionnaire, rate the first 10 movies (from top to bottom and left to right) suggested to you by “Film Buddy” (you are encouraged to apply filtering first). You can also find it here: https://goo.gl/forms/WkjXH6H3l6klyYWI3
  5. When you finish the questionnaire, you’re done! Thank you for your time! You’ve brought my graduation a step closer!
